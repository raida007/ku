MESSAGE FROM DIRECTOR

Welcome to the Research and Innovation Centre (RIC).

The Research and Innovation Centre (RIC) is playing a crucial role in accomplishing Khulna University's stated aim and vision by improving the university's research infrastructure and encouraging successful research among faculty members and research scientists. Acquiring new information has become critical in growing intellectual ability, which is the foundation for a knowledge-based developed society in today's competitive global economy. RIC continues to promote research at the highest level across all university courses and fields through various programs and activities. RIC also creates policies, strategies, and recommendations for research and research-related issues including ethics and integrity. The Research Grant Program (RGP) also provides direct funding to renowned research initiatives through a competitive process.

We appreciate your valuable support and honest engagement in fostering a knowledge-inclusive society by advancing research and communicating findings for the larger benefit.
