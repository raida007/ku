About Khulna University

Khulna University (KU) is a public research university located in Khulna, Bangladesh. Established in 1991, it has grown to become one of the leading universities in Bangladesh, known for its commitment to academic excellence, research innovation, and producing skilled graduates who contribute significantly to the nation's development.
