Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must fulfill the following requirements:

  • Must have passed SSC/equivalent in 2019 or later and HSC/equivalent in 2021 or later
  • Minimum GPA of 6.5 (combined SSC and HSC) with at least 3.0 in each examination
  • For Science Group: At least GPA 3.5 in Mathematics and Physics in HSC
  • For Commerce Group: At least GPA 3.5 in Business Management and Accounting
  • For Humanities Group: At least GPA 3.5 in relevant subjects

A Unit (Science, Engineering & Technology School)

For candidates from Science background

Subjects: Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Computer Science

Test Format: MCQ (100 marks) + Written (50 marks)

B Unit (Life Science School)

For candidates from Science background with Biology

Subjects: Biology, Chemistry, Physics

Test Format: MCQ (100 marks) + Written (50 marks)

C Unit (Arts, Humanities & Social Science Schools)

For candidates from all backgrounds

Subjects: General Knowledge, English, Bengali

Test Format: MCQ (100 marks) + Written (50 marks)

D Unit (Business Administration School)

For candidates from all backgrounds

Subjects: Mathematics, English, General Knowledge

Test Format: MCQ (100 marks) + Written (50 marks)
