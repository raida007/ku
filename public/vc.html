Role and Responsibilities

The Vice-Chancellor is the principal academic and administrative officer of the University and serves as its chief executive. Key responsibilities include:

  • Providing strategic leadership and vision for the university
  • Representing the university to external stakeholders and partners
  • Overseeing all academic, administrative, and financial functions
  • Implementing decisions of the University Council and Senate
  • Leading the development and execution of the university's strategic plan
  • Managing the university's resources to ensure long-term sustainability
