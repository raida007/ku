Research at Khulna University

Research Focus Areas

Khulna University is committed to advancing knowledge through innovative research across diverse disciplines. Our research initiatives address regional and global challenges while contributing to sustainable development.

Science and Technology

Focusing on technological innovations, environmental science, and sustainable solutions for the southwestern region of Bangladesh.

  • Environmental Science Research
  • Information Technology and Computing
  • Mathematics and Statistics
  • Biotechnology and Genetic Engineering

Social Sciences and Humanities

Investigating social, cultural, and economic aspects of development, with special focus on the unique context of the Sundarbans region.

  • Social Science Studies
  • Economic Development Research
  • Cultural and Historical Studies
  • Language and Communication Research

Life Sciences and Agriculture

Addressing challenges in agriculture, fisheries, forestry, and biodiversity conservation in the coastal ecosystems.

  • Agricultural Innovation Research
  • Aquatic Resources Management
  • Biodiversity and Conservation
  • Forestry and Environmental Management
