Vision, Mission, and Objectives

VISION OF THE UNIVERSITY

To be a center of excellence for transforming learners to self-inspiring, responsive, innovative and globally competent leaders.

MISSION OF THE UNIVERSITY

M1. Explore learners’ fullest potential to transform into self-motivated, aspiring, enterprising, inquisitive, freethinking, and creative graduates with highest ethical standards and critical thinking ability.

M2. Ensure a transformative learning environment to mutate the fullest potentiality of the learners.

M3. Create an inclusive research environment that enables learners to innovate and translate new knowledge and innovation with highest moral values and professional integrity.

OBJECTIVES OF THE UNIVERSITY

  • To explore intrinsic brilliance in learners integrating individualized learning and appropriate assessment strategies. (M1)
  • To provide collaborative learning opportunities through multi-, cross-, and trans-disciplinary exchange programs. (M2)
  • To promote cohesive academic environment that encourage aspiration, inquisitiveness, enterprise, analytical reasoning of the learners. (M2)
  • To encourage national and international collaboration for creation of new knowledge and cutting-edge research addressing local and global emerging issues. (M3)
  • To enhance academia-industry collaboration for mitigating gaps between university, acquainting students with real life situations and encourage research addressing emerging needs. (M3)
